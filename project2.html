My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia is one of the most well known anime in the world right now and for the past few years. Its a show about a super human society where about 80% of the population has some sort of ability or as they call it a "quirk".


In this new society the most popular job that has arisen is the job of being a Pro Hero because with everyone having powers many people use it to commit crimes so heros must stop thugs and other people commiting crimes.

Top 3 Pro Heros and top 3 hero agencies.

    2. Endeavor
    3. Hawks
