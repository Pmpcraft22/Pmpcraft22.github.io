The many mysteries of Jack Stauber

Jack Stauber

Jack Stauber is a very interesting man who creates many music and animated shorts on various platforms. He is my favorite artist and is single handedly the most unique artist and person I've ever seen. His music and sounds he makes are amazing and the coolest thing is that he has two different titles. Jack Stauber is his music that seems normal to the average person and then he has Jack Stauber's Micropop.

Jack Staubers top 3 songs

  1. Buttercup
  2. Oh Klahoma
  3. Dead Weight

Jack Stauber's Micropop

Jack Stauber's Micropop is his secondary form of music which takes an entire new approach towards making sounds from his main title. The best way to describe how it sounds is to say he sings in Cursive which sounds amazing.

Jack Stauber's Micropop top 3 songs

  • Two Time
  • Baby Hotline
  • Coffee

The iceberg for Jack lore dives as deep as you can imagine, the man is a living mystery from often looking dead when he performs to making many many questionable videos that have, at first glance, no meaning.
